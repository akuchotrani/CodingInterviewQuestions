What is IP or IP address?

IP is an abbreviation of Internet protocol. 

Function:
It serves two purposes
1) Identification of host/network interface.
2) Provide location of host in the network.

Version:
There are two versions of IP
IPv4 - 32 bit address. (example : 172.16.254.1)
IPv6 - 128 bit address. (example :  2001:db8:0:1234:0:567:8:1)(Developed because of growth of internet and deplition of available IPv4 addresses)

Additional Info:
IP addresses space is managed globally by IANA(Internet Assigned Numbers Authority) and 5 RIRs(Regional Internet Registeries).
